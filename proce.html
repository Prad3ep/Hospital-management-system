ADMISSION PROCEDURE

Admission in the hospital is done through OPD and Casualty for both general and private patients.

Through Casualty (Unstable Patients)

Seen by Casualty Medical Officer / Specialist.

If needs to be admitted, bed availability is confirmed from admission / reception both in SGRH/affiliated hospitals.

If bed is likely to be available shortly, patient is made to wait in Casualty under supervision of the doctors.

On availability of the bed, patient is admitted.

If there is no availability of the bed, patient is appropriately referred to other hospital.
