CHARGES

Registration card charge for private OPD is ₹150.

MRD charges and dietician charges are applicable for each episode of admission.

Charges for doctor’s consultation, operation, procedures, investigation, etc. are as per the room category.

Patients of category 1D/ Cat-2, discharged directly from the Labour waiting room will be charged according to category-2 bed charge.

In case the patient is shifted from lower to higher room category, the charges for the consultant visit, investigation, operation, will be according to the higher category from the date of admission.

In case of shifting to ICU/ICCU/PICU bed charges are as per the category in which the patient is admitted.

In case of direct admission to ICU/PICU/NICU, charges will be as per category 1D by default.
